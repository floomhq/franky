## LinkedIn Draft

```text
People keep asking the same question in different words:

"I just started building with AI. Where do I begin?"

I felt it myself while building with agents.

Then I reviewed 16 discovery calls with solo builders, Cursor/Claude users,
AI-agency operators, ops teams, and people building internal tools.

The same pattern kept showing up:

1. The builder has 5-10 workflow ideas.
2. The prototype works locally, or the idea is clear enough to start.
3. The next step becomes prioritization, env vars, secrets, auth callbacks,
   deploy targets, staging, smoke tests, and rollback.

So I made Franky.

Franky is a free AI building coach.

It helps you map workflows, score ICE, pick the first v1, stage risky
connectors, and get from local prototype to a shareable URL.

The proof spine:
16 discovery calls reviewed, 9 strong ICP fits, 5 repeated blockers extracted.

Send a workflow list or repo.
I will give you the fastest sane next step with evidence.

No platform pitch.
No fake production-ready badge.
Just workflows, versions, staging, blockers, and the next command.
```
